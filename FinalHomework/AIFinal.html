Artificial Intelligence.

We've heard the phrase for decades, but why is it just now coming into mainstream real world use? Not all A.I. are created equally. Some exist to do one thing, to do it well, and do it over and over again. Others will perform various tasks and even begin to improve how well they are able to perform those tasks as they learn over time.
